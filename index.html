Biyahe Now Brand Identity

The Biyahe Now logo represents our commitment to providing reliable, efficient, and modern transportation services throughout the region. Our brand identity reflects innovation, trust, and accessibility in public transportation.

Design Elements & Meaning:

  • Blue Color: Represents trust, reliability, and professionalism in our services
  • Orange Accent: Symbolizes energy, enthusiasm, and the warmth of Filipino hospitality
  • Typography: Modern and clean font that ensures readability across all platforms
  • Van Symbol: Direct representation of our core transportation service

Brand Values:

Our logo embodies movement, connectivity, and progress - core values that drive our mission to revolutionize public transportation. It represents our dedication to bridging communities, connecting people to their destinations safely and efficiently, and embracing technology to enhance the travel experience for everyone.

The design symbolizes our vision of a connected Philippines where transportation is accessible, reliable, and digitally integrated for the modern Filipino traveler.
